Our Mission

Trippies was born from a simple idea: every woman deserves to explore Cairo with absolute confidence and peace of mind. Our platform is a dedicated, secure, and community-driven space specifically designed to empower female travelers.

The Challenge

Lack of Privacy

Difficulty finding travel groups or workshops that are exclusively female for cultural or personal comfort.

Information Fragmentation

No single verified platform exists for girls-only travel services, leading users to rely on unorganized social media posts.

Outdated Social Proof

A lack of real-time, authentic updates on a trip’s atmosphere.

Why Trippies?

Safety First

Prioritizes user security with a "Safety Button" for instant location sharing.

Vibe Checks

Allows registered users to share temporary, 24-hour visual updates during trips for real-time transparency.

Automated Booking

A seamless, error-free booking engine for girls-only trips and workshops.

Powered by Modern Tech

  • Frontend: Flutter (Dart) for building a cross-platform, high performance mobile UI.
  • Backend: Firebase (BaaS) for real-time database management and authentication.
  • Database: Cloud Firestore to store collections for destinations, trips, and users.
